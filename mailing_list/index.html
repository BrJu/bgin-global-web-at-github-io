Mailing Lists

Join the BGIN discussion on our mailing lists.

General Discussion mailing list : General conversations about the BGIN including

  • General administrative matters
  • Meeting and events
  • Community development
  • Collaboration with other initiatives/organizations
  • Future potential work items
  • Any other topics which are out of scope of working groups and study groups

Announcement mailing list : Administrative members post info about upcoming meeting, events and other news.

GWG mailing list : Discussions related to Governance Working Group. Check here for further details about GWG.

SGIP mailing list : Discussions related to IAM, Privacy and Key Management Study Group. Check here for more info about SGIP.

Zulip Chat

Zulip is a powerful and easy-to-use communication tool that enables mailing-list like people grouping. The attendees in a stream can post messages with a specific subject to create a thread (topic).

We experimentally use the Zulip for our real-time discussion. You will be sent a invitation to join our Zulip channel after you subscribe to the General Discussion mailing list. The attendee needs to use the subscribed email address for the registration.
