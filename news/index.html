  • BGIN online meeting will be held on June 18, 13:00-15:00 UTC

      • strategy to develop the community base of BGIN

    • Meeting agenda, discussion materials and Zoom link will be sent to the registered address, but we will call for agenda items. Please let us know any agenda items to be discussed at the meeting
